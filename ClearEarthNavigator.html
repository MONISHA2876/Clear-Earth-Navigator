Problems Caused by Improper Waste Management

🌊

Water Pollution

Improper waste disposal contaminates water sources, affecting marine life and making water unsafe for human consumption.

🌬️

Air Pollution

Burning waste releases toxic gases and particulates into the atmosphere, contributing to respiratory diseases and climate change.

🦠

Disease Spread

Accumulated waste becomes a breeding ground for bacteria, viruses, and pests, leading to various health problems in communities.

🐠

Marine Damage

Plastic waste in oceans harms marine animals through ingestion and entanglement, disrupting entire ecosystems.
